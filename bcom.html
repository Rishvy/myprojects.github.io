Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

Welcome to the Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) program at VIT University, where the world of business and finance unfolds before you. Our program is designed to equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the dynamic field of commerce.

Core Business Fundamentals: The B.Com program begins with a solid foundation in core business disciplines such as accounting, finance, marketing, and economics. You'll gain a comprehensive understanding of business operations and management.

Financial Expertise: Dive into the world of finance, learning about financial analysis, investment, and risk management. You'll develop the skills to make informed financial decisions and contribute to the financial health of organizations.

Practical Insights: Our program emphasizes practical learning through case studies, projects, and internships. You'll apply your knowledge to real-world business scenarios, honing your problem-solving and decision-making abilities.

Global Perspective: In an increasingly interconnected world, you'll explore global business trends and international trade. You'll be prepared to engage with the challenges and opportunities of the global marketplace.

Your Path to Success: Join us in building a strong foundation for a successful career in commerce. Whether your aspirations lead you to accounting, finance, marketing, or entrepreneurship, the B.Com program at VIT opens doors to a world of opportunities.
